Happy Birthday Julie...

tum meri zindagi ka sabse beautiful hissa ho

ek aisi feeling ho jo explain nahi hoti

aaj ka din sirf tumhara nahi

yeh din mera bhi hai

kyunki is din meri duniya paida hui thi

tum.

tumhara hona...

mere liye kisi miracle se kam nahi hai

kabhi socha bhi nahi tha

ki main kisi se itna deeply jud jaunga

par phir tum mil gayi

aur sab kuch change ho gaya

tumhari smile, tumhari awaz

sab kuch feel hota hai andar tak

main kabhi best nahi tha

par tum mujhe better bana rahi ho

roz thoda aur

tum door ho par dil ke sabse kareeb ho

ik baat kehni thi...

main tumse bas pyaar nahi karta

main tumhe jeeta hoon

tum meri comfort ho, meri shanti ho

aur haan...

tum meri sabse badi weakness bhi ho

tumhare bina sab blank sa lagta hai

aur tum ho toh sab full of life

tumhari har cheez chahiye mujhe

har version, har mood

tum mujhe jaisi bhi ho, waise hi chahiye

tumhara gussa bhi pyaara lagta hai

kyunki woh bhi tum ho

main chahta hoon

har birthday tumhaara saath hoon

tumhe hug karu, aur bolo

"main hoon na, hamesha ke liye"

aur tum muskurake kehna...

"I know, stupid."

aaj wish karte waqt

main sirf yahi maang raha hoon

ki tum kabhi door na jao

kabhi kam na ho tumhara pyaar

aur ek din...

tum apna birthday mere surname ke saath manao

itna hi khwab hai mera

Happy Birthday meri jaan, Julie

main tumse har din thoda aur zyada pyaar karta hoon

aur karta rahunga... jab tak saans hai

tum meri ho, sirf meri.
